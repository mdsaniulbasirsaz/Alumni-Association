Why Became a Member

We are delighted to extend an invitation for you to become a member of the Rupdia Shahed Smrity College Alumni Association. As a member, you will be part of a vibrant community of fellow alumni who share a common bond with the college. You will have the opportunity to connect with fellow alumni, stay updated on the latest news and events, and contribute to the growth and development of the college.

Stay Connected

Alumni memberships provide a platform to stay connected with former classmates, professors, and the overall university community. It allows individuals to maintain relationships forged during their academic years.

Social and Cultural Events

Regular social and cultural events provide members with opportunities to network, share experiences, and build lasting relationships. These events also celebrate diversity and foster a sense of community.

Access to Resources

Members can utilize a vast array of online and offline resources, including research papers, industry reports, and educational materials. These resources are designed to support ongoing learning and professional growth.

Professional Development

Opportunities for professional development are abundant, with workshops, seminars, and courses aimed at enhancing skills and knowledge in various fields. These events are tailored to help members stay competitive in their careers.

Networking Opportunities

Our alumni association offers exceptional networking opportunities through events, workshops, and social gatherings. These help members connect with industry professionals, mentors, and fellow graduates to explore career advancement and build valuable relationships.

Access to Alumni Directories

Members can connect with former students through comprehensive alumni directories, facilitating mentorship, collaboration, and career opportunities. This network helps in maintaining professional and personal connections long after graduation.
